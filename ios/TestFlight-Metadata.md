## Beta App Description

STEW is a music creation and learning studio for songwriters, musicians, and
bands. Write with an AI-assisted songwriting workspace, jam with playable
instruments and backing tracks, collaborate on Band projects, practice ear
training, visualize musical ideas, and build skills through music games.

## What to Test

Please explore the complete STEW experience on iPhone and iPad:

- Create and continue a conversation in Songwriting.
- Select an instrument, play notes, and use backing tracks in Jam.
- Sign in to Band, create or open projects, view members, and test project
  navigation, tracks, mood-board items, and notifications.
- Complete Ear Training exercises and verify playback controls lock correctly.
- Generate and adjust instrument views in Visualizer.
- Open both games and play Harmonic Sudoku and Melody Memory.
- On iPad, rotate between portrait and landscape and try narrower multitasking
  widths. Confirm controls remain visible and navigation state is preserved.

Please report crashes, clipped or overlapping layouts, audio interruptions,
unresponsive controls, sign-in problems, lost navigation state, and unexpected
behavior when rotating or resizing the app.

## Beta App Review Notes

Most features can be tested without an account. The Band collaboration area is
the only feature that requires Sign in with Apple. Reviewers can still access
Songwriting, Jam, Ear Training, Visualizer, and Games without signing in.

The build supports both iPhone and iPad. iPhone is portrait-only. iPad supports
portrait, upside-down portrait, both landscape orientations, Split View, and
Stage Manager.
